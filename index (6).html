Recommended sleep by age

Under 13  ·  9–12 hours

Growing bodies need extra deep sleep for brain development and growth hormone release.

13–17  ·  8–10 hours

Teens have a delayed circadian rhythm — later bedtimes are biological, but 9hrs is the goal.

18–35  ·  7–9 hours

Peak performance window. Chronic under-sleep raises cortisol and kills focus and mood.

36–50  ·  7–8 hours

Sleep quality matters more than quantity now. Prioritize deep and REM cycles.

50+  ·  7–8 hours

Sleep architecture shifts. Earlier bedtimes and naps can maintain cognitive health.
